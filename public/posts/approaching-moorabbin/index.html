Finding Carrum

From the training area, fly along coast. Carrum is at major outlet/inlet from the bay.

Proceedure

  • Decend to 1500
  • Trim aircraft to reduce workload
  • Listen to ATIS on 120.9
  • Transponder to 3000
  • Tune to 118.1 for GMH, 123.0 for Carrum
